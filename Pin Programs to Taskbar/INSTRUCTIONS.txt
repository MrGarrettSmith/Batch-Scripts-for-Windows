Run .bat to pin apps to taskbar.

---------------------------------------

Pin To TaskBar
Website: https://www.technosys.net/products/utils/pintotaskbar
Github: https://github.com/0x546F6D/pttb_-_Pin_To_TaskBar

Command Line Usage:

> pttb Path\to\.exe\or\.lnk\to\PinToTaskbar
> pttb -u Path\to\.exe\or\.lnk\to\UnPinFromTaskBar
> pttb -r (Refresh taskbar by Pinning & UnPinning itself, in order to reflect changes made to:
           HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\Taskband)
